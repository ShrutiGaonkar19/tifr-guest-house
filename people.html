The TIFR Guesthouse Staff

TIFR Hostel and Guesthouse facility is serviced by a dedicated group of people who provide round-the-clock support to this facility, working in three shifts.


They are coordinated through the front desk of TIFR Guesthouse House. You can reach them for service requests or emergencies by dialing 3050 from an internal phone, or 22783050 from outside TIFR.
