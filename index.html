TIFR Guest House

Comfortable Accommodation for Official Visitors of TIFR

Welcome to TIFR Guest House

The TIFR Guesthouse building in the Colaba Campus is named after the great Indian mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan. It consists of three different categories of rooms.


In addition, the facility provides fully furnished and serviced studios and two-bedroom apartments on campus for long-term guests. These are offered primarily to official visitors of the Institute.


Rooms can be booked online using TIFR Datanet by authorized members. For all others, booking requests should be sent to the guesthouse in-charge. Please refer to the usage rules for booking policy details.

Accommodation Categories

A-Type Room

Single room with one bed and shared bathroom & toilet.

C-Type Room

Single room with two beds and attached bathroom & toilet.

D-Type Room

One room with two beds with attached bathroom & toilet, kitchenette, hall and living area.

Billing Information

Guest is advised to check with the host whether payments are to be made directly by them or handled by the host.


For room rents as well as any other charges such as food or bottled water, guests must settle the bill at the reception before checking out.


All guests are requested to inform the reception in advance about any unscheduled departure to avoid delay during settlement.

Reception Area

Guest Reception

The reception desk serves as the primary point of contact for guests during check-in and check-out. Our staff is available to assist visitors with accommodation, billing queries, room information and other guesthouse services.


Guests may also obtain WiFi credentials, local information and assistance regarding their stay from the reception desk.

Additional Facilities

Conference Room

A conference room accommodating 10–12 people is available with advance booking.

Seminar Hall

A seminar hall with seating capacity of 40–45 people is available for meetings and institutional events.
