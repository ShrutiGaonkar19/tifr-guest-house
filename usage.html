Usage Rules

Guidelines for Guests at TIFR Guest House

A Few Simple Rules for Our Guests

In order to provide efficient services to all our guests, a few simple instructions and regulations have been formulated by the management committee.


All guests and their hosts are requested to read and adhere to these rules. This helps us maintain the facility efficiently and provide uninterrupted services to all visitors.

General Guest House Rules

Official Visitors Only

The TIFR House is part of TIFR and is used only for short and medium-term official visitors to the Institute.

Maximum Stay

Guesthouse accommodation can be provided for a maximum period of 3 months.

Booking Closure

Bookings close 24 hours after arrival time for guests from India and 48 hours after arrival time for international guests.

Check-In / Check-Out

Check-In: 12:00 Noon
Check-Out: 10:00 AM

Overstay Not Permitted

Guests must vacate rooms before check-out time even if departure is delayed.

Booking Confirmation

Guests should reconfirm their booking duration with reception during check-in.

Unauthorized Guests

Guests are not permitted to accommodate unauthorized persons in their rooms.

Energy Conservation

Please switch off ACs, lights and fans, and lock rooms when leaving.

Alcohol Prohibited

Consumption of alcoholic drinks within the Guesthouse is strictly prohibited.

Valuables

Please use the electronic lockers provided. The Guesthouse is not responsible for valuables left unattended.

Billing Responsibility

Hosts must ensure that visitors are informed about bill settlement before departure.

Feedback & Suggestions

Guests may submit suggestions or complaints through the feedback form or email.

Noise Restrictions

The TIFR Guesthouse is intended to provide a peaceful environment for all guests.


Watching television or playing music at high volume is not permitted at any time and especially after 10:00 PM.


Guests are requested not to gather in corridors or disturb other residents, particularly during night hours.

Navy Nagar Regulations

Since TIFR is located within the Navy Nagar area, guests must comply with regulations imposed by the Indian Navy.


Photography is permitted only within the TIFR Housing Complex and is strictly prohibited on surrounding streets and within Institute premises.


Naval security personnel may screen or question visitors entering Navy Nagar.


Guests are strongly advised to carry a valid photo ID at all times. A temporary guesthouse ID card will also be provided during check-in.


Guests are requested to cooperate fully with security procedures and present identification whenever required.
