How To Reach Us

TIFR Guesthouse House is located inside the TIFR Housing Colony on 2, Homi Bhabha Road, opposite the TIFR campus.

To reach us, you can take a taxi and ask the driver to take you to the Navy Nagar Bus Terminus in Colaba.

Alternatively, take Bus No. 3, 11, or 125 from CST, or Bus No. 137 from Churchgate Station and get down at the last stop.

Proceed further towards the end of the road where the TIFR campus is located.

For more information visit: www.tifr.res.in
