Dining Facilities at Ramanujan Guest House

Catering Services

The Colaba campus of TIFR has several eateries which collectively provide services from 8:00 AM in the morning to 2:00 AM in the early morning for staff, residents and guests.


In addition, the guesthouse provides breakfast (included in room rent) and dinner (on request) services in the in-house dining hall at Ramanujan House.

Time: 8:00 AM – 10:00 AM
